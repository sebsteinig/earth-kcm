KCM-earth

a visualization of climate simulations

made with the Kiel Climate Model

 

boundary conditions correspond to

Early Cretaecous time period (125-110Ma)

 

all variables shown represent annual means

averaged over the last 500 years of integration

 

inspired by and build on

Cameron Beccarios "earth"
